How You Can Support Us

Your support would mean the world to us all, and there are many ways you can help support us now and into the future. Roosevelt Academy is being built through the generosity of a community that believes every student deserves the opportunity to thrive. Whether through financial contributions or donations of gently used resources, we'd be so grateful.

Donate items

Give New Life to Items Your Family Has Outgrown.

Many families have things sitting in closets that could make a huge difference, such as:

  • Board games and puzzles
  • LEGO® sets and building toys
  • Arts and crafts supplies
  • Sensory toys and fidgets
  • Bean bags and flexible seating
  • Tablets and laptops
  • Sports equipment
  • Kitchen items for your Life Skills Apartment
  • Books
  • Musical instruments
  • Gardening tools
  • Furniture
  • Small appliances

If you believe you have items we could use, please let us know.

Community involvement

Special Skills & Community Involvement

Our students learn best through meaningful, real-world experiences—and our community plays an important role in making that possible. If you have a special skill, hobby, trade, or profession you'd be willing to share with our students, we'd love to hear from you!

We also welcome connections with local businesses and organizations that may be interested in hosting class visits, offering behind-the-scenes tours, or providing opportunities for life-skills practice in real-world settings.

If you have an idea, talent, or community connection you'd like to share, please contact us.

Sponsorship

Business & Corporate Sponsorship

Corporate partnerships play a vital role in helping Roosevelt Academy create innovative learning experiences and expand opportunities for students with diverse learning needs. We welcome financial sponsorships, in-kind donations, products, services, and employee volunteer support from businesses that share our commitment to empowering students to build meaningful, independent futures.

As a Corporate Sponsor, your organization will be recognized as a valued partner in our mission. We are proud to showcase our sponsors through our website, social media, school events, marketing materials, and our growing community of families, educators, supporters, and community partners. Together, we can create lasting opportunities for students while demonstrating your company's commitment to inclusion, education, and community impact.

Many employers also offer charitable matching gift programs that can multiply the impact of employee donations. If your company participates in a matching gift program, we encourage you to explore how your support can make an even greater difference for Roosevelt Academy.

*Matching gifts will be available once Roosevelt Academy's nonprofit status is established and recognized by participating employers.

Events & fundraisers

Events & Fundraisers

At Roosevelt Academy, we believe the best memories are made when a community comes together around a shared purpose.

Throughout the year, we'll host a variety of events designed to bring our families, friends, supporters, and local businesses together. Our goal is simple: make giving back something everyone looks forward to.

Every event is an opportunity to connect, make memories, and celebrate the incredible students and families who inspire our mission. Whether you're attending, volunteering, sponsoring, donating, or simply cheering us on, you're helping create opportunities that directly impact our students' education and future.

We know there are countless worthy causes, so we want supporting Roosevelt Academy to be more than writing a check. We want it to also be an experience, and a place where our community comes together, has fun, builds lasting friendships, and celebrates every milestone along the way.

Stay tuned on our social for future events!
