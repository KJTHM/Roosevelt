Donate

Help us build something extraordinary.

Roosevelt Academy is being built through the generosity of a community that believes every student deserves the opportunity to thrive. There are many ways to help — financial contributions, donated items, skills, and community connections all make a real difference.

Financial support

Personal Financial Contributions

If you feel called to support our mission and are in a position to do so, we would be deeply grateful for your financial contribution. Every gift, regardless of size, helps us create a learning environment where students can discover their strengths, build independence, and prepare for meaningful futures.

Donate items

Give new life to items your family has outgrown.

Many families have things sitting in closets that could make a huge difference for our students. If you have any of the following, we'd love to hear from you:

  • Board games and puzzles
  • LEGO® sets and building toys
  • Arts and crafts supplies
  • Sensory toys and fidgets
  • Bean bags and flexible seating
  • Tablets and laptops
  • Sports equipment
  • Kitchen items for life skills
  • Books
  • Musical instruments
  • Gardening tools
  • Furniture and small appliances

If you believe you have items we could use, please reach out using the contact form on our Programs page.

Community involvement

Special skills & community connections.

Our students learn best through meaningful, real-world experiences — and our community plays an important role in making that possible. If you have a special skill, hobby, trade, or profession you'd be willing to share with our students, we'd love to hear from you.

We also welcome connections with local businesses and organizations that may be interested in hosting class visits, offering behind-the-scenes tours, or providing opportunities for life-skills practice in real-world settings.

Business & Corporate Sponsorship

Corporate partnerships play a vital role in helping Roosevelt Academy create innovative learning experiences. We welcome financial sponsorships, in-kind donations, products, services, and employee volunteer support from businesses that share our commitment to empowering students.

As a Corporate Sponsor, your organization will be recognized as a valued partner through our website, social media, school events, and marketing materials.

Matching gifts will be available once Roosevelt Academy's nonprofit status is established and recognized by participating employers.

Events & fundraisers

More than writing a check.

Throughout the year, we'll host events designed to bring our families, friends, supporters, and local businesses together. Every event is an opportunity to connect, make memories, and celebrate the incredible students who inspire our mission.

Whether you're attending, volunteering, sponsoring, or simply cheering us on — you're helping create opportunities that directly impact our students' education and future.

Stay tuned on our social media for upcoming events!
