Programs & services

An education shaped around your child.

From the first preschool days to the leap into adulthood, Roosevelt Academy meets students at every age. Each program pairs individualized academics with on-site therapy, and need-based scholarships keep cost from standing in the way.

For our youngest learners · Ages 2–5

Early Childhood Program

A warm, play-based preschool where developmental goals hide inside songs, stories, and sensory play. Early-intervention specialists and therapists help each child build the foundations of communication, movement, and connection, at their own pace.

For elementary students · Grades K–5

Elementary Academy

Real academics, including reading, math, science, and art, taught with an individualized learning plan for every student. Lessons are adapted to how each child learns, so progress feels possible and school feels like a place they belong.

For middle schoolers · Grades 6–8

Middle School Program

The in-between years, supported. Students keep building academics while we add social-emotional learning, friendships, and the first real practice at independence, all in a setting that understands the bumps of growing up differently.

For high schoolers · Grades 9–12

High School Program

Academics, electives, and a growing sense of independence. Students explore their interests and strengths while we help them prepare, academically and personally, for whatever comes after graduation.

For young adults · Ages 18–22

Transition & Vocational Program

The bridge to adulthood. Students practice job skills, independent living, money management, and community navigation through real-world experiences and partners who help them step into a future of their own.

Woven into every program · All ages

Integrated Therapy Services

Speech, occupational, and physical therapy delivered right inside the school day, with no separate appointments and no extra drives. Our licensed therapists collaborate with teachers so support follows your child from the classroom to the playground and back.

For the whole family · All ages

Family Partnership & IEP Support

Your child's education is a team effort. We keep families close with regular updates, IEP guidance, and a staff that helps you navigate schools, services, and funding so the support continues long after the final bell.

Admissions

Reaching out is the first step. We made it simple.

There's no intimidating application to start. Just a conversation. Tell us a little about your child and the best way to reach you, and a real member of our admissions team (not an automated system) will follow up within two business days to schedule a tour.

Not sure if Roosevelt Academy is the right fit? Reach out anyway. If we're not the right school for your child, we'll gladly point you toward someone who is.

Your child's information is always kept private and secure. We never sell or share family details, and only the admissions staff supporting you will ever see what you submit.
