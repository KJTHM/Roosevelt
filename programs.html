Programs & services

An education shaped around your child.

Roosevelt Academy currently serves students in 8th through 12th grade. Every program pairs individualized academics with life skills and vocational readiness, and need-based scholarships keep cost from standing in the way.

Grades 8–12 · Core academics

Individualized Academic Program

Real academics — reading, writing, math, science, and more — taught through an individualized learning plan built around each student. Lessons adapt to how each student learns, so progress feels possible and school feels like a place they belong.

All grades · Daily practice

Executive Functioning & Life Skills

Organization, planning, self-regulation, and the practical skills of adult life — from managing a schedule to cooking a meal. These skills are woven into every school day, not treated as an afterthought.

All grades · Social growth

Social & Emotional Development

Building friendships, navigating relationships, communicating needs, and developing the emotional tools to handle challenges — all in a setting that understands how our students experience the world.

Grades 10–12 · Future readiness

Vocational Exploration & Readiness

Students explore career interests, build job skills, and gain real-world experience through community partnerships. We help every student identify their strengths and take confident steps toward meaningful employment.

Woven into every program · All grades

Integrated Therapy & Support Services

Speech, occupational, and physical therapy delivered inside the school day — no separate appointments, no extra drives. Our licensed therapists collaborate with teachers so support follows each student from the classroom to the real world.

Life beyond graduation

Graduation isn't the finish line, it's the beginning of what's next.

As Roosevelt Academy grows, our vision extends beyond high school. In future phases, we hope to develop post-graduation opportunities that support young adults as they continue building independence, confidence, and meaningful lives through continued education, vocational training, employment partnerships, life-skills development, and community engagement.

Our goal is to help every student transition into adulthood with purpose, dignity, and the support they need to thrive.

Admissions

Reaching out is the first step. We made it simple.

There's no intimidating application to start. Just a conversation. Tell us a little about your student and the best way to reach you, and a real member of our team will follow up within two business days to schedule a visit.

Not sure if Roosevelt Academy is the right fit? Reach out anyway. If we're not the right school for your student, we'll gladly point you toward someone who is.

Your child's information is always kept private and secure. We never sell or share family details, and only the admissions staff supporting you will ever see what you submit.
