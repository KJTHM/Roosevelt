A school built around how your student learns.

Roosevelt Academy is a nonprofit day school in Austin for students in grades 8 - 12 with disabilities and diverse learning needs. We pair individualized academics with life skills, vocational readiness, and on-site therapy, so each student is taught the way they learn best.

Now welcoming founding families for grades 8 - 12 in Austin.

We teach students the way they actually learn: one plan, one student at a time.

Roosevelt was founded by Austin families and educators who wanted a high school built around students with disabilities, instead of one that asks them to fit a mold.

Our approach

We build the school around the student.

Every student at Roosevelt has an individualized learning plan, small classes, and a team that knows them well. Real academics (reading, writing, math, and science) sit alongside executive function, life skills, and vocational readiness, every day.

We start with what a student can do and build from there: confidence, independence, and the academic and life skills they will use after graduation.

Read our story

What families can count on

  • Small classes. A low student-to-staff ratio so every student is truly known.
  • A team that knows your student. Educators who learn how your student thinks, communicates, and works.
  • Therapy on site. Speech, occupational, and other supports built into the school day, with no separate appointments.
  • Need-based scholarships. We work to keep cost from standing between a family and the right school.

What your support covers.

As a new nonprofit school, Roosevelt runs on the support of its community. Here is what a gift pays for.

$50: a day of school

Covers a full day of individualized learning and on-site therapy for a student whose family needs help with tuition.

$250: adaptive learning tools

Provides communication devices, sensory supports, and adaptive technology that help a student access the lesson.

$1,000: a scholarship season

Helps cover a season of need-based tuition, so a student can keep their seat no matter what.
