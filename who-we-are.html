Who we are

A school built by families, for families.

Roosevelt Academy began with a simple, stubborn belief: no child should be turned away from a classroom for learning differently, and no parent should have to fight for a place where their child belongs. Everything we teach grows from that.

Our story

It started with one borrowed classroom.

That one room became Roosevelt Academy. Today we still believe one great classroom, with one teacher who truly knows each child, can change everything. Small by design, personal by conviction.

We don't ask children to fit the classroom. We build the classroom to fit the child.

— The Roosevelt Academy promise

Mission & values

What we stand for

Our mission: to give every child with special needs a real education in a place where they are safe, known, and free to become exactly who they are.

The child first

Every lesson begins with one question: is this right for the learner in front of us? Never a label, never a number.

Trust & privacy

Families share their child's hardest moments with us. We honor that with confidentiality, honesty, and care, always.

Belonging for all

We welcome every child and every family, across every ability, background, and budget. Need-based scholarships keep cost from being a barrier.
