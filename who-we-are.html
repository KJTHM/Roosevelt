About Us

About Roosevelt Academy

At Roosevelt Academy, every student gets an education designed around who they are — not around a standardized system.

Founded by parents and educators in Austin, Texas, with firsthand experience navigating the challenges of special education, Roosevelt Academy was built around one idea: when you design school around the student instead of asking the student to fit the school, real progress happens. We recognize that every learner has unique strengths, interests, abilities, and goals. Instead of expecting students to fit into a predetermined curriculum, we design learning experiences that meet them where they are and help them move confidently toward what they are capable of achieving.

Our mission extends beyond earning a diploma. We are preparing students for work, independence, and adult life — through individualized academics, communication, executive functioning, social development, life skills, vocational exploration, community engagement, and real-world experiences.

Success here is measured by each student's own progress and growing independence — not by comparison to anyone else.

Our Vision

Our Vision

We are building a school where students with diverse learning needs get an education designed around them — one that equips them with the academic, vocational, and life skills to move into adult life with confidence.

Families are part of the process. Students are known as individuals. The program grows alongside each learner.

Our Phased Approach

Roosevelt Academy is intentionally being built in phases to ensure we create a thoughtful, high-quality program that grows alongside our students.

Phase One

Our inaugural program will serve students in 8th through 12th grade, providing highly individualized education focused on academic growth, executive functioning, communication, life skills, vocational readiness, and preparing students for successful transitions into adulthood. This initial phase allows us to build the personalized culture and programming that are at the heart of Roosevelt Academy.

Looking Ahead

Our vision extends well beyond high school.

Future phases include transition, vocational, and adult learning programs that continue after high school — helping young adults build real employment skills, continue learning, and stay connected to their community.

Roosevelt Academy is a school and a community — one that stays with students past graduation, into the work and adult life that comes next.

Our Story

Our Name & Mascot

Why Roosevelt?

Our name was inspired by Theodore Roosevelt, not because he lived an easy life, but because he demonstrated what is possible when determination, courage, and purpose come together.

Despite facing significant personal challenges, Roosevelt accomplished more in a remarkably short period of time than many accomplish in a lifetime. He believed in action over excuses, in perseverance over limitations, and in creating opportunities where others saw obstacles.

That spirit became the foundation of our school.

Every student here is seen for what they can accomplish — not defined by a diagnosis, a disability, or a standardized expectation. Like Roosevelt, our students have strengths waiting to be discovered and built on. That's what we're here for.

Growth begins when you find the right environment.

— The Hermit Crab Principle

Our Mascot

The Hermit Crab Principle

Growth begins when you find the right environment.

Just as a hermit crab periodically searches for a new shell that better fits its growth, every student needs an environment where they feel safe, understood, and challenged in the right ways. At Roosevelt Academy, the environment adapts to the student — not the other way around.

We also have to admit there was a second reason the hermit crab felt like the perfect mascot...

Many of our students are perfectly content being "hermits" every now and then. They often recharge in quiet spaces, prefer smaller groups, and flourish in environments where they feel safe and understood. We don't see that as something to change. We see it as something to respect.

At Roosevelt Academy, students grow at their own pace and step forward with new skills and confidence when they're ready.

Our Philosophy

Every Student. Their Own Path.

At Roosevelt Academy, education starts with who the student is — how they learn, what they need, where they're going. Our approach is rooted in connection and flexibility, because growth looks different for every child.

Students do their best work when they feel safe, understood, and appropriately challenged. That's why we build individualized learning experiences around each student's strengths and give them the structure to take real steps forward.

The philosophy is plain: meet students where they are, and help them build the academic, social, and life skills they'll actually use.

Partnership

Families & Educators: One Team, One Goal

At Roosevelt Academy, the strongest outcomes come from partnership between families and educators.

Parents know their children better than anyone. They understand what motivates them, what challenges them, what brings them joy, and what success looks like beyond the classroom. Our educators bring professional expertise, creativity, and a commitment to individualized learning. Together, we create something neither could accomplish alone.

Education shouldn't happen in isolation. It's a collaborative process — families, educators, therapists, and support professionals working together with one shared purpose: helping each student make real progress toward their own goals.

Communication is at the heart of that partnership.

Families can expect regular updates, real conversations, collaborative goal setting, and shared celebrations of progress. Parents stay connected to what's happening — not through a newsletter, but through the people teaching their child.

As students grow, we also invite them into the conversation. Learning to advocate for themselves, make choices, and participate in setting goals is an essential part of developing confidence and independence.

At Roosevelt Academy, we are a team with one focus: when families and educators work together, students make real progress.

Founding families

Seven families. Seven students. One school.

The Roosevelt Academy was founded by seven families and seven incredible students who inspired us to stop waiting for the "right" school and start building it ourselves.

Like so many parents of kids who learn differently, we had experienced the frustration of trying to fit our students into systems that weren't built with them in mind. We knew there had to be another way — one that built on each student's strengths, developed real independence, and prepared them for adult life on their own terms.

Our founding families didn't want to settle for "good enough." They chose to advocate, to aim higher, and to create something that didn't yet exist.

Our students each have different abilities, personalities, and paths. What they share is this: every one of them deserves a school that takes them seriously.

Today, those seven students are at the heart of everything we do. They remind us why Roosevelt Academy exists and why we'll continue growing.
